A Letter to You

Dear friend,

I wanted to tell you something that's been on my mind. Like atoms forming stars, like particles dancing in the cosmic void, you bring meaning to my world.

There's something beautiful about connections that don't demand explanation. They just exist, quietly powerful, like gravity itself.

I hope you know that you matter. Not because of what you do, but because of who you are. Every moment with you is a moment worth remembering.

Thank you for being part of my universe.

With gratitude,
A Friend
